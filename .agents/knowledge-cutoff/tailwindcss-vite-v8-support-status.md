# @tailwindcss/vite — Vite 8 Support Status

> Researched: 2026-03-14

- Latest stable: `4.2.1` (2026-02-23) — peer dep `^5.2.0 || ^6 || ^7`, does **not** support Vite 8
- Vite 8 support was merged to `main` on 2026-03-12 (same day as Vite 8 release), but not yet tagged
- Watch for `v4.2.2` or `v4.3.0`
- Open issue (#19792): possible LightningCSS config conflict between Vite 8 and the Tailwind plugin

**Implication:** Upgrading to Vite 8 is blocked until a new @tailwindcss/vite release ships.
